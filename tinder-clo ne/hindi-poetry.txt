/////what is a lover to me :

qyamat-e-tufaan me bhi vo muskurate the ,
aashiq kaho ya muzafir ,
ye vo the jo kadam kadam pe musibato se takrate the...


Mujhe pyaar teri rooh se hai,
Jism ka tere mai kya karunga,
jo janat de khuda tere badle ,
vaha bin tere mai kya karunga...
Jiss Khuwab me tu nahi vo neend nahi chahiye mujhe,
jaha tera ashk hi nahi aise duniya me mai kya karunga...

Unke lie unki nazro se bhi girna gawara hai hame ,
Bas gawara nahi ki unki takleefo k kaaran bane...



////The respect in a relationship :

naam na dena in lamho ko aashiqi ladane ka ...

Pyaas pyaar ki hai hame ,
Jismo ka loobh nahi ,
Ishq ki qadar karte hai hum ,

///When it hits breakups :

Shayad issi lie har zuba ki dua ban gye...
lavso par apna sach baya karte karte ,
na jaane kab hua yeh,
aashiq the ab shayar ban gye...

Teri kamyabi me saath mera bhi tha ,
aur mai jo tutta usme haath tera bhi tha ,
Takleef hogi tumko ye baat manne mai ,
Par saath jo hamara chuta ,
nuksaan usme teera bhi tha....


jinka har dard dur ho jata tha hamare aane se,
ab vo pehchanne se bhi katrate hai ,

neendo mai khuwab dekha karte the jinka ,
ab vo yaad bhi nahi aaya karte hai....
